The Singapore Math syllabus chatbot




This is the Singapore Math syllabus GenAI chatbot. It is created using Google's Vertex AI Conversation and Dialogflow CX. The data is obtained from the 2020 Secondary Math syllabus document. Thus there might have been revisions to the syallabus since.

Click on the dialog box button at the bottom right to chat with the chatbot. You may test it out with the following prompts below for a start.

  • What is inside the Secondary Math syllabus?
  • What is metacognition?
  • What are the big ideas?
  • what is inside the NA-Level Sec 2 geometry syllabus?

As I am currently using free trial credits provided by Google Cloud, the chatbot will cease once it reaches the credit limit or expires on June 10, 2025, whichever comes first.
